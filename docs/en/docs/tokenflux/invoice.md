# Invoices

TokenFlux currently supports `invoice` documents and mainland China general invoices. Different invoice types are handled differently, so choose the option you need.

## Supported Types

- **Invoice**: Automatically generated when you pay through Stripe. No admin contact is required.
- **Mainland China general invoice**: Contact `daoge` for manual processing. A `6%` service fee applies.

## Invoice Entity

The invoice title provided by this site is: `深圳市萌凛科技有限责任公司`.

## How to Apply

If you need a mainland China general invoice, contact `daoge` and provide the following information:

1. Required invoice type: mainland China general invoice.
2. The order or top-up record that needs an invoice.
3. Invoice title, tax ID, and other required details.

After the information is confirmed, manual processing will be assisted.
