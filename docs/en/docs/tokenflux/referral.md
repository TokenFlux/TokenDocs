# Referral Rewards

Invite friends to use TokenFlux. After an invited user makes their first purchase, the inviter immediately receives `30🍥` as a reward. The reward is sent directly to the inviter's account balance.

## How to Find Your Referral Link

1. Open the [profile page](https://tokenflux.dev/profile).
2. Find the referral-related link and invitation code at the bottom of the page.
3. Copy the referral link or invitation code and share it with friends.

After a friend registers through your referral link and makes their first purchase, the reward is automatically sent to your account.

## Reward Rules

- After an invited user makes their first purchase, the inviter immediately receives `30🍥`.
- Rewards go directly to account balance and can be used for future purchases.
